Formulas of  Physics , Maths and Chemistry

Maths 10 formulas

  1. Area of Circle:
    A = πr2
  2. Circumference of Circle:
    C = 2πr
  3. Pythagoras Theorem:
    a2 + b2 = c2
  4. Quadratic Formula:
    x = (-b ± √(b2 − 4ac)) / 2a
  5. Simple Interest:
    SI = (P × R × T) / 100
  6. Slope of Line:
    m = (y2 − y1) / (x2 − x1)
  7. Distance Formula:
    √[(x2 − x1)2 + (y2 − y1)2]
  8. Area of Triangle:
    A = ½ × b × h
  9. Volume of Cube:
    V = a3
  10. Mean:
    Mean = (Sum of observations) / n

Chemistry 5 formulas

  1. Molarity:
    M = moles of solute / litre of solution
  2. Density:
    ρ = m / V
  3. Ideal Gas Equation:
    PV = nRT
  4. pH =
    −log[H+]
  5. Avogadro’s Number:
    1 mol = 6.022 × 1023 particles

Physics 5 formulas

  1. Speed:
    v = d / t
  2. Acceleration:
    a = (v − u) / t
  3. Newton’s Second Law:
    F = m × a
  4. Kinetic Energy: KE = ½mv2
  5. Ohm’s Law: V = I × R
